Qbila for Government Organizations

Qbila is geolocation based public information system designed and developed to achieve Persistent, Complete and Effective Government-Citizen communication.
Higher Impact at Lower Cost Achieve greater and effective outreach at a significantly lower cost in comparison to any traditional or modern internet based medium.
Share actionable information. Share more than information. Add actions to information. Designed to be an extension to the official internal information infrastructure.
Multimedia support Package information with multimedia. Add more interactivity with images, videos, graphs, maps etc.
Citizen engagement analytics for better understanding and decision making. Qbila is inherently a geograhic information system. Realtime action oriented collaboration with citizens and better understanding of response.
Priority of issues can be decided with citizens using upvoting and commenting. Giving another perspective to enable decision for impactful action using implementation tools or policy tools of governance.
Targetted Communication Officials can share information with specific groups based on location and demography.

Qbila for Citizens

One application for all government information and communication
Unprecedented Level of Communication We are working towards persistent, complete and effective Government-Citizen communication, that has never been achieved in history of democratic governments.
Know what is happening around you, how it affects you and how can you contribute towards making things better for everyone.
Follow multiple locations you live at or care about. Get information feed from government organisations and people living in these places.
Customized information feed to meet your information needs. We understand that needs of citizens can be different. Add you personal information and get information filtered to suit your needs.
Prioritize issues around you. Vote up or share what should be worked on around you to improve lives of everyone. These issues will be followed up till resolved.
Actionable Information for active participation in governance. Once you know, know what to do about it.

Qbila for Researchers

Open Documents Facts and documents backed journalism and analysis. We believe open documents can reduce costs of local data driven journalism.
Open Data, The data originated from Government-Citizen interaction will be open to study and analysis for non-commercial purposes.
GIS Add meaning data with our Geolocation based data and visualisations.
Write and Share Issue centric research can be shared with auhorities and people.
